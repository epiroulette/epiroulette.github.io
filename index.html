Your one stop modern TV remote

Yellow provides you with a central access to all your favorite TV shows.

Supporting a heavy stack of famous platforms such as Netflix, Prime Video, Hotstar and Sony Liv, Yellow is a new modern TV remote that helps you binge your all time favorite shows, keep up to date on all the live sports matches, and provides you with a collection of Yellow's weekly top 10 shows/movies to binge.

How To Use

1. Scan through the roulette to find a show. Use the arrow buttons provided, or the arrow keys on your keyboard for navigation.

2. Use 'Quick Watch' to binge episodes that are played randomly across all seasons of the show.

3. Use 'Sports' to find all currently live sports matches being broadcasted or catch-up on highlights.

4. Use 'Top 10' to pick from Yellow's top 10 picks of shows and movies.
